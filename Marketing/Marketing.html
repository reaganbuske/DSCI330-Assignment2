Marketing offers a wide variety of interesting and challenging career options, so we’ll give you the tools and resources you need to be successful, no matter which direction you choose to follow. You’ll learn how to create, communicate, capture and deliver value to customers, and to manage relationships in ways that benefit both businesses and clients in a socially responsible manner. The WSU Marketing Program in the College of Business will help you develop these capabilities through a progressive curriculum, relevant hands-on experiences, and state-of-the-art technology. You’ll have unique real-world opportunities to expand your classroom experiences with our marketing internships, and our highly qualified faculty will be there with you to share their extensive experience and education.
